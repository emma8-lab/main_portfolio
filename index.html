About Me

I am a motivated Biological Sciences graduate with strong research experience in microbiology, molecular biology, and infectious diseases. Skilled in antimicrobial resistance studies, molecular techniques, and data analysis. Proven track record of teaching assistance, academic presentations, and leadership roles. Passionate about advancing research on host-pathogen interactions and contributing to public health knowledge.

My Skills

Laboratory: PCR, microbial culturing, biochemical assays, light microscopy, genome-wide analysis

Data Analysis: SPSS, R, STATA, XLSTAT, Excel

Communication: Scientific writing, oral presentations, report preparation

Languages: English (fluent), Fante, Twi, French

Research Experience

Research Laboratory Assistant (Intern) Abakrampa Health Center October 2021 - December 2021
  • Conducted bacterial isolation and characterization of Klebsiella pneumoniae
  • Performed antimicrobial susceptibility testing and biofilm formation studies
Undergraduate Thesis Project Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST) January 2023 - September 2023
  • Led research on ESBL-producing E. coli in raw beef using biochemical and PCR techniques
  • Presented findings at national veterinary conference
